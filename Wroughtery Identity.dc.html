02The mark & the system

The hot iron is the signal

A reduced anvil — long tapered horn left, low splayed base — caught at the moment of forging, an ember billet resting on its face. That billet is the system rule: hot = the umbrella, an active or AI tool; a bare cold anvil = an ordinary tool.

04Color

Iron and ember, with a cool counter-tone

Iron carries the type and most surfaces. Ember is the hot iron — used sparingly, for the billet, a single call to action, and AI. Steel keeps the palette off the warm default; paper is a cool off-white, never cream.
